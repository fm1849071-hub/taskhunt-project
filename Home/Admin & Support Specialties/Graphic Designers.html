Frequently Asked Questions

What does a graphic designer do?

Graphic designers create visual content such as logos, branding, social media posts, banners, brochures, and marketing materials.

How much does it cost to hire a graphic designer?

Pricing depends on design type, complexity, number of revisions, and the designer’s experience level.

What design tools do graphic designers use?

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Figma, Canva, and other professional design tools.
