Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a Legal Advisor and a Lawyer?

A legal advisor focuses on providing strategic guidance, risk assessment, and contract reviews to prevent legal issues, while a lawyer typically represents you in court.

Can I get a written legal opinion?

Yes, you can request a formal written legal memo or opinion from your advisor as part of your consultation or as a fixed-price task.
