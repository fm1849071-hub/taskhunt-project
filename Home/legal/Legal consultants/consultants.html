Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical rate for a Senior Legal Consultant?

Consultancy rates reflect the depth of expertise. Strategic advisors typically start from $100/hr, while top-tier subject matter experts may range up to $450/hr for complex corporate restructuring or IPO advisory.

Can I book a one-off strategic session?

Yes. You can book a single "deep-dive" session to discuss a specific legal risk, or engage a consultant for ongoing monthly advisory to oversee your business's legal health.
