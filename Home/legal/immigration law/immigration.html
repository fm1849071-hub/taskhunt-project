Frequently Asked Questions

Can an immigration professional guarantee my visa approval?

No ethical lawyer or consultant can guarantee an approval, as the final decision rests with the government. However, our experts maximize your chances by ensuring your file is 100% compliant and presenting your case in the strongest legal light.

What is the difference between a Lawyer and a Migration Agent?

An Immigration Lawyer has a full legal degree and can represent you in court appeals. A Migration Agent is a licensed specialist (like RCIC in Canada or MARA in Australia) focused specifically on visa applications and government processing.
