Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a Lawyer and a Legal Assistant?

A Legal Assistant (Paralegal) handles legal research, document drafting, and administrative support but cannot represent you in court or provide independent legal advice like a licensed lawyer.

Can an assistant help with my court filings?

Yes, many of our senior assistants are experts in e-filing systems and can manage the submission of documents to various court portals efficiently.
