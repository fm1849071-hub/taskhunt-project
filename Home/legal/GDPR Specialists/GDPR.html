Frequently Asked Questions

Does my company need GDPR compliance if we are based outside Europe?

Yes. GDPR applies to any business, regardless of location, that processes the personal data of individuals located in the EU. If you have European users or customers, you must comply.

How can a GDPR specialist help avoid the 4% global turnover fine?

Specialists conduct Gap Analysis, implement data mapping, and set up incident response plans. These proactive steps demonstrate "good faith" to authorities and can significantly reduce or eliminate potential penalties.
