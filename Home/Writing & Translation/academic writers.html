Frequently Asked Questions

What types of academic writing do you cover?

We cover all academic levels from high school to PhD, including essays, research papers, theses, dissertations, literature reviews, case studies, and lab reports across all disciplines.

How do you ensure the quality of academic papers?

All writers have advanced degrees in their fields. Each paper goes through multiple quality checks including plagiarism detection, grammar verification, and formatting review.

What citation styles do your writers know?

Our writers are proficient in all major citation styles including APA 7th, MLA 9th, Chicago/Turabian, Harvard, IEEE, and Vancouver. Just specify your requirement.

Can you help with urgent deadlines?

Yes! We have writers available 24/7 for urgent projects. We can handle deadlines as tight as 3-6 hours for shorter papers and 24-48 hours for longer research work.
