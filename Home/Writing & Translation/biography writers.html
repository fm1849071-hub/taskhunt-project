Frequently Asked Questions about Biography Writing

How do biography writers capture my story if I'm not a writer?

They conduct in-depth interviews (online or by questionnaire) to gather stories, memories, and key events. They then weave these into a compelling narrative using their writing expertise.

Can you write a biography about a family member who has passed away?

Absolutely! This is called a tribute or memorial biography. Writers can work with photos, letters, and interviews with relatives to create a beautiful legacy piece.

What's the difference between a memoir and a biography?

A biography covers a person's entire life story, usually written by someone else. A memoir focuses on specific themes or periods in someone's life and is often written by the subject themselves (with a ghostwriter).

How long does it take to write a full biography book?

It depends on the length and complexity. A short family history (50 pages) might take 4-6 weeks. A full book (200+ pages) typically takes 3-6 months. We can discuss timelines with your chosen writer.
