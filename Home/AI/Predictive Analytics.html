Frequently Asked Questions

What is Predictive Analytics?

Predictive analytics is the use of data, statistical algorithms, and machine learning techniques to identify the likelihood of future outcomes based on historical data.

How accurate are the predictions?

Accuracy depends on data quality and volume. Our experts provide confidence intervals and error metrics (like MAE or RMSE) to quantify uncertainty.

What industries benefit most from this?

Finance (Credit scoring), Retail (Inventory), Healthcare (Patient risk), and Marketing (Churn/Conversion) are major beneficiaries.
