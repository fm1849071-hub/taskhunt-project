Frequently Asked Questions

What are Machine Learning Models?

They are programs trained on a set of data to recognize patterns and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed for that task.

How much data do I need?

The amount of data depends on the complexity of the problem. Simple models may need hundreds of rows, while deep learning requires thousands or more.

Can you help with model deployment?

Yes, our developers can deploy models using Docker, AWS SageMaker, or as simple APIs using Flask or FastAPI.
