Frequently Asked Questions

What is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to models that can create new content—text, images, audio, or video—based on the data they were trained on.

What is Fine-tuning?

Fine-tuning is the process of taking a pre-trained model (like GPT-4) and training it further on a smaller, specific dataset to improve performance for a particular task.

Can I run these models on my own servers?

Yes, our experts can help you deploy open-source models like Llama-3 or Mistral on your private infrastructure for total data control.
