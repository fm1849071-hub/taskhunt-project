Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Data Science and Data Engineering?

Data Engineers build the "plumbing" (pipelines/storage), while Data Scientists use that data to build "models" and insights.

What cloud platforms do your engineers use?

Our experts work across AWS (S3/Redshift), Microsoft Azure (Data Factory/Synapse), and Google Cloud (BigQuery).

Can you help migrate our on-premise data to the cloud?

Yes, our specialists are experienced in secure data migrations and cloud-native architecture setups.
